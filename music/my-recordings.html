My recordings

I am not a singer. I have never in my life taken a singing lesson, and I'm sure it shows. I've barely even practiced; I don't sing when other people are around. But I have found it to be quite fun to sing when nobody is around, and I guess I may as well make the recordings available here, where I'm not forcing anybody to listen. Besides, if you sing a folk song badly, that just makes it all the more authentic.
